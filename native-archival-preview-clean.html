Our Story

Hand crafted. Brand building. Made in America.

At Pulp Packaging Inc.—the makers behind Native Archival—our story is built into every box we create.

It began in 1906, when H.R. Drake & Sons first crafted boxes in the very factory we still call home today. As decades passed, the company evolved, becoming part of Paul T. Freund Corp. and growing through the integration of many respected box-making businesses—all united under one roof along the historic Erie Canal in Palmyra, New York.

More than a century later, while names and ownership have changed, one thing has remained constant: who we are.

We are box makers.

Craftsmen who believe the contents deserve respect—and that the box should reflect their value.

120 Years of Craft

1906
HR Drake & Sons Company begins manufacturing boxes in Palmyra, New York, on the banks of the Erie Canal. The factory that still stands today.
1950s
Paul T. Freund Corp. acquires HR Drake & Sons, beginning a decades-long consolidation of box-making expertise under one roof.
1970s
The factory expands, acquiring and integrating multiple specialty box makers. Acid-free and archival-grade materials become a core focus.
2000s
Pulp Packaging Inc. consolidates all operations into the historic Palmyra facility. The company now produces a full range of premium packaging and archival products.
2018
Native Archival launches, bringing factory-direct archival products to photographers, collectors, and archivists. Direct-to-consumer pricing. Made in the USA.
Today
Over 120 years of box-making heritage. Still family-operated. Still made in the same factory. Still protecting what matters.

What We Stand For

◆

Made in the USA

Every product is manufactured in our Palmyra, New York factory. We don't outsource. We don't cut corners. American made, from start to finish.

◆

Archival Quality

Acid-free, lignin-free, PAT-tested. Our materials meet the standards set by museums and professional archivists for long-term preservation.

◆

Direct to You

No middlemen. Factory-direct pricing means you get professional-grade archival products at a fair price, shipped straight from our facility.

Policy

Shipping & Returns

Last updated April 2026

Shipping

All orders are processed and shipped from our factory in Palmyra, New York. We typically process orders within 1–2 business days. During peak seasons or promotional periods, processing may take an additional 1–2 days.

Domestic Shipping (United States)

Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days. Rates calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

Expedited Shipping: 2–3 business days. Available at checkout for an additional fee.

Free Shipping: Available on orders over $75 within the contiguous United States.

International Shipping

We currently ship to Canada and select international destinations. International shipping rates and delivery estimates are calculated at checkout. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to monitor your shipment’s progress at any time.

Shipping from our factory direct to you

Because we manufacture and ship from the same facility, your order goes straight from our production floor to your door — no middlemen, no warehouses, no unnecessary handling.

Returns & Exchanges

We stand behind the quality of every product we make. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are happy to help.

Return Window

You may return unused, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be in their original condition and packaging.

How to Initiate a Return

To start a return, please contact us at custservice@nativearchival.com with your order number and reason for return. We will provide you with a return shipping address and any further instructions.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. Please allow an additional 3–5 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your order arrives damaged or you received a defective product, please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damage. We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you, including return shipping.

Non-Returnable Items

Custom or personalized orders cannot be returned unless they arrive damaged or defective. Sale items are final sale unless otherwise noted.

Questions?

Contact our customer service team at custservice@nativearchival.com or call 315-502-4265. We’re here to help.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You may also have the right to opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any marketing email or by contacting us directly.
